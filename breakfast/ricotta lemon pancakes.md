# Ricotta Lemon Pancakes

Makes pancakes for 2.

- 1 cup ricotta cheese
- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1+1/2 tbsp sugar
- 1 pinch of salt
- 3/4 cup milk
- 2 large eggs
- 1 lemon zest
- butter, for cooking
